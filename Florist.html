About Us

Rooted in Champaran, Blooming with Passion

We are a dedicated team of floral artists serving the beautiful districts of West Champaran and East Champaran in Bihar. From Bettiah to Motihari and surrounding areas, we bring creativity and elegance to every celebration.

Whether you desire fresh seasonal flowers or high-quality artificial arrangements that last forever, we customize every detail to match your vision and budget. Our expertise spans traditional Indian wedding decor to modern minimalist corporate setups.
